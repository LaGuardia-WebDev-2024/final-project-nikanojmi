About Me

Nika Nodjoumi is a 17 year old musician and songwriter. Her main passions include songwriting and playing drums in different genres, but she also plays guitar, piano and sings. Nika is a current senior at Laguardia high school. She is going to Loyola Univeristy for jazz and pop commercial music studied with a minor of music industry. Music means everything to Nika.

Nika plays jazz, rock, classical. At laguardia high school she is part of the new music ensamble, Jazz orchestra, wind symphony and gospel choir.
